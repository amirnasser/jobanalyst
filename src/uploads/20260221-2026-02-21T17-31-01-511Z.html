Job Title: Angular Developer

Location: Mississauga, Ontario, Canada

Duration: FTE

Experience: Mid to Senior

Salary: CAD 110k/p.a. - 120k/p.a.

A skilled Angular Developer responsible for building scalable, high‑quality web applications, driving end‑to‑end feature delivery, ensuring UI performance, and collaborating closely with stakeholders to deliver robust front‑end solutions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform feasibility analysis, effort estimation, and support IT planning and risk assessment.
  • Design, develop, test, and deploy Angular applications across all SDLC phases.
  • Own end‑to‑end delivery of assigned features from development to production.
  • Analyze complex technical and business problems and propose effective solutions.
  • Ensure application security, performance optimization, and post‑implementation improvement.
  • Collaborate with business users and cross‑functional teams to resolve issues and enhance functionality.
  • Drive adherence to development standards, best practices, and coding guidelines.
  • Work independently with minimal supervision and act as SME for team members and stakeholders.
  • Recommend and implement UI/UX performance enhancements.

Technical Skillset

  • Strong experience building enterprise‑scale web applications using Angular 16+ (minimum needed) or Angular 18+ (Good to have).
  • Proficient in JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, JSON, Ajax.
  • Design and implement Angular Micro Frontend architecture using Module Federation, enabling independent deployment and scalable modules.
  • Excellent knowledge of advanced Angular concepts: components, services, DI, routing, lazy loading, forms, observables, RxJS operators.
  • Skilled in unit testing frameworks for Angular.
  • Ability to convert Figma designs into functional Angular components.
  • Experience integrating RESTful APIs with Angular applications.
  • Hands‑on experience with Git, JIRA, and Agile/Scrum delivery.
  • Exposure to Micro UI architecture and Angular library development.
  • Familiarity with uDeploy/Jenkins for CI/CD automation.
  • Working knowledge of MongoDB/Oracle databases.
  • Strong foundation in system analysis, architecture, and SDLC best practices.
